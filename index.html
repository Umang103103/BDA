Company: From Beginning – Till Date

1974 Building Design Authority (P) Ltd., a multi-disciplinary Nepali consultancy, was established in 1974 as Building Design Associates Pvt. Ltd.
1984 Reconstituted in 1984 (2040/10/09) as Building Design Authority (P) Ltd., becoming one of the pioneer Nepali full-time consulting firms.
2000 BDA expanded into other construction sectors beyond buildings, including infrastructure and engineering projects.
